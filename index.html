About Me

Holly is a licensed Dental Hygienist and has been very successful in the field of dentistry. Holly has a very driven, determined and a want to succeed. After 16 years of dental hygiene, Holly has decided to pursue a career in computer coding.

Holly grew up with computers starting with a 1986 Mac and has only evolved her knowledge of computers from there. She enrolled in the University of Minnesota- Coding Bootcamp in the fall of 2023. Holly is expanding her knowledge of computers and is inspired to learn and take on the role of a full stack web developer. She is an excellent communicator and a very supportive team player. Holly strives to exceed expectations in all aspects of her career.
